# Supplier FAQ — Carbonfact for Suppliers

Use this reference when answering supplier questions during outreach, preparing for calls, or handling objections. These are the real questions suppliers ask.

---

## What is Carbonfact?

Carbonfact is a sustainability data platform used by **200+ apparel and footwear brands** (On, Carhartt, The North Face, Marc O'Polo, GANNI) to measure, compare, and reduce product footprints.

## What is Carbonfact for Suppliers?

A public directory where brands find innovative, lower-impact materials and processes. Selected suppliers get a **free profile** and a page per material, plus the ability to securely share environmental data with brands. It solves two problems: brands struggle to find credible, comparable data, and suppliers struggle with discovery and safe data sharing.

**Demo video**: https://www.carbonfact.com/hubfs/Airops%20Product%20Image/Supplier.carbonfact.com%20launch.mp4

## What are the benefits for suppliers?

Being listed is **free** and enables you to:
- **Control** environmental data sharing — share selectively, control access per brand
- **Save time** — no more PDFs, NDAs, and email back-and-forth per brand
- **Become Carbonfact verified** — Science team reviews and normalizes your LCAs at no cost (results kept confidential)
- **Receive inbound requests** from brands actively sourcing lower-impact materials
- **Be discoverable** via Google, ChatGPT citations, and inside Carbonfact's eco-design tools (200+ brands)
- **Get promoted** — featured on social media and in newsletter sent to 5,000+ brands

## How does verification work?

Carbonfact verifies and normalizes your LCAs so results use consistent indicators, clear system boundaries, and documented sources (PEF-aligned; ISO 14040/44). This makes comparisons fair and gives verified suppliers a competitive advantage.

Quality requirements must be met for credibility. Third-party critical review is a **nice-to-have, not mandatory**. The process:
1. Share existing LCA docs confidentially with Science team (NDAs available)
2. Science team reviews and verifies at no cost
3. Verified data added to your profile (supplier profile + dedicated page per material)
4. Brands discover your materials and request access
5. You approve or deny each request
6. Only after your approval can a brand view the data

## How is data handled and kept confidential?

- LCA documents hosted securely, never publicly accessible
- **You control who views your data** — brand-by-brand approval via dashboard
- Data used solely for environmental assessment and modeling
- Protected under Carbonfact's data security safeguards: https://trust.carbonfact.com
- Sharing via Carbonfact is **more secure than PDF by email** — brands see verified data and system boundary only, not the actual LCA document
- You can revoke access at any time

## How can brands request access?

1. Brand fills request form on your material page (includes brand name + reason)
2. You receive the request and decide whether to share
3. If approved, brand sees data and can run eco-design simulations (if Carbonfact client)
4. You can remove their access at any time

## What are the sharing options?

- **1-to-1** with specific brands upon request (default)
- **All Carbonfact customers** (200+ brands)
- **Public** — everyone

## If I share with all Carbonfact customers, can they redistribute my data?

**No.** Third-party data remains the intellectual property of the original provider. Carbonfact clients may use it only within the platform for internal purposes (footprint calculations, product LCAs, reports). Terms of Service prohibit extraction, copying, or external distribution without written consent.

## Is it free? What does it cost?

**Yes, free.** Listing, claiming your profile, and LCA verification are all free. If you need a completely new LCA and want to commission the Carbonfact Science team, that's a paid service (usually cheaper than traditional consultancies).

## How do brands discover suppliers?

- **Public search** on suppliers.carbonfact.com (findable via Google and ChatGPT)
- **Inside Carbonfact's modeling tools** — teams test material/process swaps in their products
- **LinkedIn announcements** when new suppliers go live

## Do you replace NDAs?

Carbonfact doesn't provide legal advice. Many suppliers find that access control and logging reduce repeated NDA/file-sending steps. You can still use NDAs if your policy requires them. Carbonfact is happy to sign an NDA for LCA review.

## Our LCA is not critically reviewed — can we still join?

**Yes.** Carbonfact will review your LCA for free. If it meets their requirements, you're added to the directory. Third-party critical review is a nice-to-have, not mandatory.
