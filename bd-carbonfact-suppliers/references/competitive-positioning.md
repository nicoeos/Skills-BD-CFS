# Competitive Positioning — Carbonfact for Suppliers

## Carbonfact vs. Vaayu

| Dimension | Carbonfact | Vaayu |
|---|---|---|
| **Focus** | Fashion/footwear-specific LCA + eco-design. Dedicated supplier platform (suppliers.carbonfact.com). | Retail-broad (fashion, beauty, resale). AI-powered automated calculations. |
| **Supplier offering** | Free supplier directory with verified LCA profiles, access control, brand discovery. Direct integration into eco-design tools used by 200+ brands. | No dedicated supplier-facing platform. Supplier data collected through brand-side workflows (Product Design Studio). |
| **LCA approach** | Product-level, SKU-level LCAs. PEF/ISO 14040 aligned. 50M+ LCA database. Science team reviews supplier LCAs for free. | AI/ML automated calculations. 600K+ data point database. Focus on speed and scale. |
| **Key differentiator for suppliers** | Suppliers get a **public-facing profile they control**, making them discoverable. Vaayu has no equivalent — suppliers are data inputs, not visible participants. |

---

## Carbonfact vs. Fairly Made

| Dimension | Carbonfact | Fairly Made |
|---|---|---|
| **Focus** | LCA calculation + carbon accounting + eco-design + supplier ecosystem. | Supply chain traceability + impact measurement + digital product passports. |
| **Supplier offering** | Free directory with LCA-verified profiles. Suppliers control access. Integrated into brand eco-design workflows. | Works with suppliers to collect traceability data, but primarily as a service to brands — not a supplier-facing directory. |
| **Strengths** | Deep LCA expertise at product/SKU level. Eco-design simulation. Dedicated supplier platform. | Full supply chain mapping (Tier 1 to raw materials). 5-criteria scoring. Digital passports + QR codes. 100M+ passports. |
| **Client base** | 200+ brands (On, Carhartt, The North Face, Columbia, New Balance). YC-backed, $15M Series A. | 100+ brands (LVMH, Versace, Balmain, SMCP, APC). €15M Series A (BNP Paribas). |
| **Key differentiator for suppliers** | Carbonfact = **marketplace-like directory** where suppliers opt in and control data. Fairly Made = brand-driven traceability where suppliers are profiled **by the brand**, not self-service. |

## Carbonfact vs. Traditional LCA Consultancies

| Dimension | Carbonfact | Consultancies |
|---|---|---|
| **Cost** | Free verification of existing LCAs. Paid LCA creation cheaper than consultancies. | €5K–€30K+ per product LCA. |
| **Speed** | Weeks, not months. | 2–6 months per study. |
| **Comparability** | Standardized, PEF-aligned. Same methodology across all results. | Methodology varies by consultant. Hard to compare across studies. |
| **Visibility** | Data discoverable to 200+ brands on the platform. | A PDF in a folder. No distribution. |
| **Ongoing value** | Profile stays live, updated. Continuous brand requests. | Static report. Outdated within a year. |

## 30-Second Differentiator (for calls/conversations)

> "Other platforms either measure carbon for brands or trace supply chains — but none of them give suppliers a profile they own and control, where brands can discover them and use their data directly in eco-design. Carbonfact for Suppliers is the only platform where your LCA data lives where sourcing decisions actually happen."
