# Email Sequences — Carbonfact for Suppliers

All emails follow the **Why / What / How** UVP framework defined in the main skill.
Every email ends with a CTA driving toward **booking the 15-min intro call**.
After FU2, or on any "not now" reply, deploy the **warm referral P.S.**

**Language adaptation**: Translate and culturally adapt these templates to match the recipient's language. French suppliers get French emails. German suppliers get German. Do not just translate literally — adapt tone and formality to local business norms.

## Warm Referral CTA — When to Deploy

| Context | Timing | Phrasing |
|---|---|---|
| After "not now" reply | Immediately in reply | "Totally understand. By the way — do you know a brand or peer supplier who might be interested?" |
| After FU2 no-reply | In FU2 P.S. (already included above) | See P.S. lines above |
| After successful onboarding | 2–4 weeks post-profile-live | "Now that you're set up — any peer factories or material innovators who'd benefit from this?" |
| LinkedIn DM fallback | After email sequence exhausted | "No worries if timing's off. Know anyone in your network who cares about environmental data?" |
